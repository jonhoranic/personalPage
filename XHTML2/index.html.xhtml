Loki's Staff
A wood working project

A Brief Explination

I am a big fan of costume and prop making, and in my free time I do DIY projects to make replicas of peices seen in nerd culture. The above image is the scale design print for the small version of a magical staff seen in the Marvel Avengers movie. This scene should help better illustrate what it should look like.


There is also a small story behind this specific project choice. I love the Marvel movies as much as the next guy, but I happen to know a girl who is really invested with this cinimatic universe. Her favorite chracter is Loki, played by Tom Hiddleston. So my reasoning for makeing this should be obvious now.


Resources

  • Many referance images and stills were taken from the movie and from set images of Hiddleston and the staff for scale. (See Referance tab.)
  • I tend to carve with dried pinewood, it is a softer wood and the grain is easy to follow. However this is just my preferance.
  • A long list of trade tools come into play, everything from hand chizels to paint brushes were needed to complete this prop. (See Tools tab)
  • Resycled parts were also needed along with resin pouring equipement, this was the most tedious part of the process.
  • Paint and sealer. A lot of paint and sealer.
